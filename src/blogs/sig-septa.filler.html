How to get to Susquehanna on SEPTA

General SEPTA tips (please read!)

  • Trains/subways are almost always more reliable than busses. Operator avaliability and traffic cause the bus system to have large unpredictable delays.
  • Get information about what time your bus/train will arrive from the SEPTA app. Google Maps is very bad with showing delays/cancellations.
  • The Transit App is another good and reliable app for transit directions, and also the easiest to use.
  • Whenever riding non regional rail SEPTA services, you receive 2 free transfers to other non regional rail SEPTA services. This means that if you take the bus/trolley/subway, you can take 2 more bus/trolley/subway rides for free within the next 2 hours. You do not need to transfer to the same mode of transit. For example: you can transfer from bus to subway or bus to bus or anything else you'd like. You must pay with a SEPTA Key or a contactless credit/debit card to receive this benefit, and you must use the same payment method for all transfers
  • The niceness/cleanliness of SEPTA's transit modes usually ranks:
    1. Regional Rail
    2. Bus/Trolley
    3. Subway

Reccommended Routes

You can take the reverse of these home

44 Bus between "15th and JFK" and "City Av and Presidential"

  • Cost: $2.00
  • Transfers: 0
  • Frequency: Fairly frequent. Should still plan ahead.
  • On-time performance: Typically OK on busses before 7:30 am and after 6:00pm.
  • Time: 10 min walk to bus + 24 min bus ride = 34 min

27 or 125 Bus between "15th and JFK" and "Wissahickon Transfer Center", then 65 Bus/Bike/Walk to "City Av and Presidential"

  • Cost: $2.00
  • Transfers: 0
  • Frequency: Fairly frequent. Should still plan ahead.
  • On-time performance: Same as 44 bus
  • Time: 10 min walk to bus + 24 min bus ride = 34 min

Manayunk/Norristown Regional Rail to "Wissahickon", then Route 65 Bus/Bike/Walk between "Wissahickon" and "City Av and Presidential"

Manayunk/Norristown Schedule PDF

  • Cost: $4.75 (bike/walk) - $6.75 (bus)
  • Transfers: 1
  • Frequency: Planning required, especially in non-peak hours.
  • On-time performance: Train leg is good, bus is usually good.
  • Time (bus): 10 min walk to suburban + 19 min train to wissahickon + 5-10 minute wait for bus + 8 minute bus ride = 42 - 47 min
  • Time (bike): 10 min walk to suburban + 19 min train to wissahickon + 19 min bike = 48 min
  • Time (walk): 10 min walk to suburban + 19 min train to wissahickon + 28 min walk = 57 min
  • Note: It's a pretty uphill walk/bike. I would avoid walking/biking unless using this route to go home

Cynwyd Regional Rail to Bala, then Bus/Bike/Walk to SIG

Cynwyd Schedule PDF

  • Cost: $4.75
  • Transfers: 0
  • On-time performance: Good
  • Frequency: Super infrequent. You have to plan to be on one of the one trains in each direction that leave at times aligned with Susquehanna's office hours.
  • Time (bus): 10 min walk to suburban + 22 min train to bala + 5-10 min wait for bus + 12 min bus = 49 - 54 min
  • Time (bike): 10 min walk to suburban + 22 min train to bala + 10 min bike = 42 min
  • Time (walk): 10 min walk to suburban + 22 min train to bala + 22 min walk = 54 min
